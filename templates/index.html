Who we are

CleanTrack is a community-focused web platform that empowers local residents to report and track waste-related problems in their neighborhoods. Whether it's an overflowing garbage bin, litter in public areas, or irregular waste collection, we help ensure such issues are heard and acted upon.

Our platform allows users to submit complaints easily, attach images, and follow real-time updates from the concerned authorities. This encourages transparency, accountability, and faster responses — making the entire process smoother for everyone.

Built as a student-led initiative, CleanTrack reflects our belief that technology can be a strong force for solving real-world problems. With a simple and user-friendly design, we aim to bring cleanliness, awareness, and civic responsibility into one digital space.

Together, let's create cleaner, healthier communities — one report at a time.
